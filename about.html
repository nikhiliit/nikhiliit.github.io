My Story (So Far...)

My journey into AI started with a simple question: "What if computers could help doctors spot diseases faster?" During my undergrad, I spent countless hours in labs at IIT Delhi, working with real medical data from AIIMS Hospital. Seeing how a small algorithm improvement could potentially help save lives - that's when I knew this was more than just code to me.


After completing my Masters at Illinois Institute of Technology and dropping my dream for PhD, I dove into industry work - first at building production computer vision systems, diffusion models. Now at Flam AI as a Senior Research Engineer, every day feels like sketching new possibilities!
